Explore the fascinating details about our Sun!

Sun Overview

Name: The Sun

Type: G-type main-sequence star (Yellow Dwarf)

Size and Radius

Equatorial Radius: 696,340 km

Diameter: 1.39 million km

Temperature

Core Temperature: 15 million °C

Surface Temperature: 5,500 °C

Sun's Energy

The Sun generates energy through nuclear fusion, converting hydrogen to helium in its core.

Distance from Earth

Distance to Earth: 149.6 million km

Solar System Influence

The Sun's gravity governs the orbits of all planets and celestial objects in the Solar System.
